Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What exactly do I buy?

You buy physical Paulownia trees on our plantation. With the tree certificate you are registered as the owner.

Are there hidden costs?

No. €249 per tree covers everything: seedling, planting, care, land lease, harvest and processing.

Can I visit my trees?

Yes, anytime. On request we organize a personal tour on site.

When are the trees harvested?

After 8–12 years. Each tree delivers approx. 1.3–1.6 m³ of premium wood.

Is this a financial product?

No. It is a physical purchase — comparable to buying land. You buy real trees, not a financial instrument.
Note: The purchase of Paulownia trees does not constitute the acquisition of a financial product. Greenchild does not make any forecasts regarding future earnings. The value of the wood is subject to market fluctuations.
